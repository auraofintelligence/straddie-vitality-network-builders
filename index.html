Evidence Boundary

The site uses cautious public wording. It supports planning and source gathering; it does not diagnose, prescribe or promise treatment outcomes.

Dementia: Australia already has a major dementia burden, with AIHW reporting 443,274 Australians living with dementia in 2025 and dementia as Australia's leading cause of death in 2023.

HBOT: This toolkit favours mask-fed PADS over whole-chamber oxygen enrichment, so gas mix and flow can be adjusted while fire, sensor and electronics safety remain visible.

Sauna: Finnish cohort studies associate frequent sauna use with lower cardiovascular and all-cause mortality, but this is not the same as proof of cure or prevention.

Toxicity: PFAS exposure is widespread, but public claims must distinguish exposure, association, risk and proven harm.
